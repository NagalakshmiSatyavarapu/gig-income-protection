# Gig Income Protection System

##  Problem Statement
Delivery workers depend heavily on daily earnings from completed deliveries. However, during extreme weather conditions such as heavy rainfall or high temperatures, delivery demand decreases significantly. This leads to reduced working hours and unstable income.

This project proposes an AI-powered parametric micro‑insurance system that protects delivery workers by compensating them for temporary income loss caused by external environmental disruptions.


##  Target Users
The primary users are food delivery workers.

- Earn per delivery  
- Income depends on demand  
- Weather affects work  
- No income protection  



##  Risk Triggers

- Heavy Rain → Rainfall > 50 mm  
- Extreme Heat → Temperature > 40°C  

##  Weekly Insurance Plans

| Plan | Weekly Premium | Coverage |
|------|--------------|----------|
| Basic | ₹20 | 3 hours income |
| Standard | ₹40 | 6 hours |
| Premium | ₹60 | 10 hours |


##  Dynamic Premium (AI)

- Low Risk → ₹15  
- Medium Risk → ₹30  
- High Risk → ₹50  

Based on:
- Weather history  
- Location risk  
- Delivery activity  

## Parametric Insurance

- Rain > 50mm → Auto trigger  
- Temp > 40°C → Auto trigger  

✔ No manual claim  
✔ Instant payout  



##  System Workflow

1. Worker registers  
2. Selects plan  
3. Location tracked  
4. Weather monitored  
5. Risk detected  
6. AI estimates loss  
7. Claim auto triggered  
8. Payout sent
